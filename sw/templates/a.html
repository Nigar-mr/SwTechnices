Voluntary Reporting

Read "Note" below before starting

NOTE : Voluntary reporting allow all SWT employees for the
submission of information related to observed hazards or inadvertent errors, safety events without an associated legal or administrative requirement to do so. When to make such a report: 1. When hazard or safety issue that may not be captured by the mandatory reporting system 2. You wish for others to learn and benefit from the actual or potential safety hazard/safety issue but are concerned about protecting your identity(confidential report) 3. You wish to report actual or potential safety hazard /safety issue openly Report will be read and validated by the SWT SMS manager. The manager may contact the reporter to make sure he understands the nature and circumstances of the occurrence/hazard reported and/or to obtain the necessary additional information and clarification.
